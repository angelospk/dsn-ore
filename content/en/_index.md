## Our voice, our city

Welcome to the official website of the **Youth Council of Orestiada**. We are a group of young people with vision, enthusiasm and ideas for our place.

### What do we do?
*   **We propose** solutions to the Municipality on issues that concern us.
*   **We organize** cultural, volunteer and sports activities.
*   **We advocate** for a better everyday life for students and young workers.

Join us to make Orestiada more youthful, more outgoing and more vibrant!
